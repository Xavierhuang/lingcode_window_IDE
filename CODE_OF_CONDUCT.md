# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for
everyone who participates in the LingCode community.

Be respectful. Harassment, discrimination, and abusive behavior are not tolerated
in any project space (issues, pull requests, discussions, and chat). Assume good
intent, give constructive feedback, and help keep the community productive.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the
maintainers at **support@lingcode.dev**. Reports will be handled confidentially.
